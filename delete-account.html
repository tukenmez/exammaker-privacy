Delete Your Account & Data

Exam Maker · Last updated: June 14, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Exam Maker account and the data associated with it. Exam Maker is developed by the Exam Maker team.

Option 1 — Delete inside the app (instant)

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open Exam Maker.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap your Account ID, then tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Deletion is immediate and irreversible; a fresh anonymous identifier is issued the next time you open the app.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer open the app, email support@cramai.app with the subject “Account deletion request”. If you still have access, include your Account ID (shown in Settings) so we can locate and erase your record. We action verified requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

DataWhat happens
Your account & anonymous identifier (UUID)Deleted
Usage counters (generation count, last-used date, subscription link)Deleted
Support messages you sent (and any email you provided)Deleted
In-app settings & saved preferencesDeleted
Uploaded documents / images / text used to generate questionsNever stored — processed in real time and discarded, so there is nothing to delete

What is retained, and for how long

  • Account & personal data: erased from our live database immediately (Option 1) or within 30 days of a verified request (Option 2). Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within a further 30 days.
  • Purchase records: subscriptions and payments are processed by Google Play (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS). Those stores retain transaction records under their own policies; deleting your Exam Maker account does not cancel an active subscription. Cancel it in your store account’s subscription settings.
